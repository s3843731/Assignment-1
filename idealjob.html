Ideal Job


Penetration Tester at Deloitte

Position

This position will include tasks that involve recognising any flaws in the security within the IT environment of clients, highlighting any possible exposures and generating strategies for rectification. Another aspect of this position is acting as a support and mentor to the other team members within the workplace. Some of the security aspects include the following; networking and host layer vulnerability assessments, firewall, networking and security device reviews, web application assessments, Application Programming Interface (API) assessments and social engineering. A reason this job is appealing to me is that the job is located in Melbourne, which is where I am currently situated, so location is not a problem. Also, Deloitte is an accounting organisation which is were I could apply both my economic and finance skills and also my IT skills, which I will gain through my tertiary studies. The position is also entry level with a full time contract, which promises a sense of job security to an extent.

To be qualified for this position, the major criteria include the following:
