Interest in IT

What is your interest in IT?

Information Technology has become such a large part of many, if not all, people's lives.
Whether it be for medicinal purposes, entertainment purposes, it plays such as large role in today's society. To me,
it is interesting how so many different things have sprouted from technology in varying ways to serve many purposes. I have
always liked playing video games from a young age, like Xbox, Wii and PlayStation, mostly action, RPG. My interest in IT
started around high school when I met a peer who had an interest in IT and had some simple programming skills,
which was very different to what I was exposed to in previous years of education. Also, I have a very close friend who did a
bachelor degree in IT and he has influenced me. I personally, do not have much IT experience, but I learnt about making
website using HTML in high school, but did not learn any further as classes were not offer.

Why RMIT?

From personal experience, RMIT has a really positive and surperior reputation. Many friends
have expressed their satisfaction with attending this institution. My father, also got his bachelor degree from RMIT.
Another reason is that the courses offered in this institution satisfy what I wish to pursue. At first, I wanted to complete a
Bachelor Degree of Business (Economics and Finance), however, I wanted a course that was more IT centred. So currently, I am studying
a Bachelor Degree of IT, with minor studies of Basic Economics and Finance. Also, the campus at which I would have been studying at
is the city campus, and I enjoy the city scenery and environment.

What do you expect to learn during your studies?

From my studies, I expect to learn some basic and advanced IT skills, including programming, networking,
coding and analytical skills that I can apply in a future career. I also expect to learn about hardware and software designing with
some security in computing aspects.
